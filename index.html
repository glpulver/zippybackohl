Before We Go Further

Three Questions We Always Get First

What does it cost to launch?

Nothing upfront — for the league or any club. ZippyBack invests in design, production, serialization and fulfillment. Clubs approve designs and earn a revenue share on every unit sold. No inventory to buy, nothing to invoice.

Who controls the brand and designs?

The league and each club control everything. Every collectible is built to each club's official marks and brand standards. Nothing is produced or sold until your club signs off. ZippyBack is the manufacturing and fulfillment partner — the identity always stays yours.

Is this a league deal or club by club?

Both structures are on the table. The program is designed to scale across all 20 clubs as a league-level initiative — with licensing revenue and a member benefit the OHL can announce. We can also onboard clubs individually and build toward a full league program.

Questions, Answered

FAQ

How does the OHL make money from this program?
The OHL earns a licensing fee on every collectible sold across all clubs in the program — a new, recurring revenue line that requires nothing from league operations. The exact fee structure is set in the league partnership agreement. Each club also earns its own revenue share independently on every unit sold through its channels.
What does it cost a club to start?
Nothing upfront. ZippyBack invests in design, production, serialization and fulfillment. Your club approves the designs and earns a share of every unit sold — there's no inventory to buy and nothing to invoice.
How does a club make money?
Your club earns a revenue share on every collectible sold across the concourse, your team store and the branded online store we run for you. The exact share, pricing and edition sizes are set with your club in the partnership agreement.
Who controls the designs and our brand?
You do. Every collectible uses your official marks and brand standards, and nothing is produced or sold until your club signs off. We're a manufacturing and fulfillment partner — your identity stays yours.
Do we have to carry inventory or run fulfillment?
No. We handle production, warehousing, shipping, customer service and re-orders end to end. For game-day, we can supply display-ready stock for your merch team to sell.
Can we do limited and special editions?
Yes — that's where it shines. Home & Away, championship and playoff commemoratives, season-ticket-member exclusives, honoured numbers, anniversary and theme-night drops are all on the table.
Is this an OHL-wide deal or club by club?
Both structures work. The program is designed to scale across all 20 clubs as a league-level initiative — with the OHL as the anchor partner and clubs participating under that agreement. We can also onboard clubs individually and build toward a full league program. We're happy to discuss what structure makes the most sense for the OHL.
Is the product proven and authentic?
Yes. ZippyBack is a patented Canadian collectible platform, individually serialized, already licensed by the NHL Alumni Association and live with golf and minor-hockey partners today.
